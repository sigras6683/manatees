Manatees are peaceful sea creatures that thrive in the shallow waters of Florida and are known as sea cows. 

In 2021, over 1,100 manatees died due to loss of habitat, boat accidents, and toxic algae blooms. Becauses of the great number of manatee deaths, it is illegal to harm, hunt, or feed manatees. They are apart of the "threatened" list of animal species.
